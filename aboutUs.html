Furnituristic

For Furnituristic, a furniture retailer, we created a website using HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript to effectively showcase their diverse product range. With intuitive search functionality and a streamlined shopping cart, the website enhances user engagement and simplifies the purchasing process. This development has significantly improved Furnituristic’s online presence, leading to increased customer satisfaction and higher sales.
